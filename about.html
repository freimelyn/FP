About Us

Nestled in one of the most peaceful beach front villages in Pagudpud near the northern tip of Luzon, Hakuna Matata offers picturesque views of awe-inspiring sunsets, swaying green palms, and white sand beaches that stretch for miles. The beautiful property, aptly named after the Swahili phrase, is the perfect spot for travellers who want to experience island life tranquility. But this doesn’t mean that you will not have a window to the outside world as Hakuna Matata rooms have flat screen television sets fitted with Netflix and Youtube through a stable fiber optic Internet access.

Warm Embrace

From the Swahili phrase “hakuna matata,” which means “no worries,” hat’s exactly what you’ll find at Hakuna Matata Beach Resort. It’s the perfect place to forget your troubles and simply enjoy the moment. Whether you’re soaking up the sun on the white sand beach, swimming in the crystal-clear waters of the South China Sea, or exploring the nearby town of Laoag, Hakuna Matata Beach Resort is sure to help you relax and unwind.
